:

Rules for Privilege of The Floor


Per Chapter A-321, Section 3-B, The rules are as follows:

  1. A speaker's time shall be limited to three minutes, which time may be extended, upon request of the speaker, at the Mayor's discretion;

  2. Speakers are to limit the content of their comments to matters concerning which City Council, on behalf of the City, has discretion or control; and

  3. Speakers shall not engage in politicking for public office.
